Government Skill Development Projects

PMKVY — Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana

PMKVY is a flagship skill development scheme aimed at providing industry-relevant training and certification to unemployed youth, thereby enhancing employability and productivity.

Key Objectives:
  • Provide industry-oriented skill training
  • Certification through Sector Skill Councils
  • Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL)
  • Encourage self-employment and entrepreneurship
Training Sectors:
  • Electronics & Hardware
  • Beauty & Wellness
  • Apparel Sector
  • Digital & Financial Literacy
Placement Strategy:
  • Dedicated placement support team
  • Strong industry partnerships
  • Regular job fairs and campus drives
  • Post-placement tracking and support

DDUGKY — Deen Dayal Upadhyay Gramin Kaushal Yojana

DDUGKY focuses on improving livelihoods of rural youth by providing structured skill training programs and assured placement opportunities across multiple sectors.

Sectors Covered:
  • Distribution Lineman (Power Sector)
  • CRM Voice / Non-Voice (IT/ITES Sector)
Mobilization Strategy:
  • Community outreach programs
  • Local mobilizers and counseling teams
  • Information, Education & Communication campaigns
  • Digital and social media awareness
Placement & Impact:
  • Dedicated placement cell
  • Corporate tie-ups for employment
  • Continuous employment tracking
  • Enhancement of rural livelihoods
