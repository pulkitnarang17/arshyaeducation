PMKVY — Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana

PMKVY is the flagship skill development scheme of the Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship (MSDE), Government of India. The objective is to provide industry-relevant skill training to Indian youth so they can secure sustainable employment and better livelihoods.

Key Objectives

  • Provide job-oriented skill training to unemployed youth
  • Bridge the gap between education and industry requirements
  • Promote entrepreneurship and self-employment
  • Certification through recognized Sector Skill Councils
  • Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL)

Training Sectors & Job Roles

  • Electronics Sector — Field Technician Computing & Peripherals
  • Mobile Phone Repair Hardware Technician
  • Beauty & Wellness — Assistant Beauty Therapist
  • Apparel Sector — Self-Employed Tailor

Additional Programs

  • Digital Literacy & IT Enabled Services
  • Financial Literacy Programs
  • Health Awareness Programs
  • Women Empowerment Initiatives

Operational Strategy

  • Infrastructure setup as per government norms
  • Hiring SSC certified trainers
  • Structured curriculum & session planning
  • Candidate mobilization & counseling
  • Regular quality monitoring
  • On-Job Training (OJT)
  • Assessment & Certification

Placement Strategy

  • Dedicated placement cell
  • Industry tie-ups for job roles
  • Tracking placement progress of candidates
  • Regular vacancy updates to trainees
  • Focus on maximum placement coverage

DDUGKY — Deen Dayal Upadhyay Gramin Kaushal Yojana

DDUGKY is a flagship program under the Haryana State Rural Livelihood Mission (HSRLM) aimed at providing skill training and employment opportunities to rural youth from economically weaker sections.

Sectors Covered

  • Power Sector — Distribution Lineman
  • IT / ITES Sector — CRM Voice & Non-Voice

Training Capacity

  • Distribution Lineman — 210 Students
  • CRM Voice / Non-Voice — 140 Students

Mobilization Strategy

  • Community engagement with local leadership
  • Mobilizers & counselors at each center
  • IEC campaigns and outreach programs
  • Social media promotion (Facebook, WhatsApp, etc.)
  • Monitoring and evaluation of mobilization

Operational Strategy

  • Training infrastructure preparation
  • Recruitment of certified trainers
  • Curriculum development
  • Training commencement & monitoring
  • OJT & practical exposure
  • Assessment & placement drives

Placement & Industry Tie-Ups

  • Placement support through dedicated team
  • Tie-ups with companies across sectors
  • Regular placement drives
  • Tracking of employment outcomes

Social Impact

  • Rural youth employment generation
  • Economic upliftment of families
  • Skill development for sustainable livelihoods
  • Support for women empowerment
